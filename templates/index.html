📚 How it works:
  1. Semantic Segmentation: U-Net identifies ROI (Region of Interest) vs RONI (Background)
  2. Differential Encoding: ROI uses high-bandwidth encoding, RONI uses low-bandwidth
  3. Data Compression: Sparse matrix compression reduces transmission overhead
  4. Channel Simulation: Transmits through AWGN or Rayleigh fading channels
  5. Reconstruction: Combines ROI and RONI to reconstruct the image
